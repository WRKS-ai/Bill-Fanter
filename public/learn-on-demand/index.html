Frequently Asked Questions

01
What's your risk to reward per trade?
I only take trades which have a 1:1 or 2:1 payout, meaning I’ll risk $1 of capital to make $1 or $2 (100% return or better).
02
How much do you risk per trade?
My average trade is either 10 or 30 contracts, which equates to $1,000-$3,000 of capital.
03
If you're only risking $1,000-$3,000 then how do you get returns of $10,000?
Options are leverage, so 1 contract is equal to owning 100 shares of stock.

If I buy 10 contracts on Apple for $1,000 it’s the same as controlling $178,000 in shares. When Apple moves $5 in a day, then the value of the options contracts can go up by 3x or more.
04
Your returns look too good to be true, so what’s the catch?
That’s the power of options! When Apple moves $2 in a day, the options contracts move by 30% or more.
05
How do you minimize losses?
I use a stop loss of 20%-30% to stop out of a trade once it invalidates from my entry point.
06
How much can I make?
All depends on your willingness to learn. My trades average a 20%-100% return on a daily basis.
07
How long did it take you to learn to be consistently profitable?
About 90 days, which is why I have all my students trade in a simulator with “monopoly” money. This way, they have no capital at risk until they know exactly what they’re doing.
08
What will I learn in your class?
Literally everything! It’s 12 hours of comprehensive training which allows students to have an incredible understanding of how to profit from the start.
09
How do I know what to buy and when?
The Masterclass comes with access to a Discord Community with buy/sell signals which are called out live while the market is open.
10
What happens after the class ends?
With the “Learn on your own” or Foundations Masterclass, you can reach out to me any time via email and I will be there for you every step of the way. For students in the Masterclass Pro (Mastery), I am here for you in perpetuity for 1:1 coaching.
11
Do I need prior experience?
No! In fact, I’d prefer you didn’t. That way I don’t have to break any bad habits you might already have.
12
How much time is required to be successful?
At a minimum, you need to invest a couple of hours a week to become proficient in learning this new skill.
13
How much do I need to get started?
You’ll need $1,000 in a brokerage account and $100-$200 a month for the software and indicators.
14
How long does your Masterclass take to complete?
It’s 6 individual sessions which last approximately 2 hours a piece. All sessions are recorded for you to replay.
