FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior trading experience?
No. The masterclass is built for beginners. We start from "what is an option?" and build up step by step. If you've been trading already, you'll move through the foundations quickly and get more value from the strategy and risk-management sessions.
How are the sessions delivered?
Six live sessions taught by me personally — with real-time Q&A. Every session is recorded and you keep lifetime access to the recordings, so you can rewatch any time.
What's the difference between Foundation and Mastery Pro?
Foundation gives you the 6 sessions plus 6 months of Discord and watchlist access. Mastery Pro adds 3 months of 1:1 personalized coaching where I review your actual trades with you, plus 12 months of community access. Mastery Pro is the right choice if you want direct guidance on your portfolio.
How much time per week should I expect?
Each live session runs about 90 minutes. Plan on 2–3 hours per week if you want to follow along live, take notes, and review the homework. The community is there 24/7 once you're enrolled.
What if I can't make a live session?
Every session is recorded and posted immediately. You can submit questions in the community channel and I'll address them in the next session or directly in chat.
Is options trading risky?
Yes — all trading involves risk, including the potential loss of principal. A huge part of the masterclass is teaching the risk-management framework I use myself, so you're never putting more on the line than you've decided you can afford to.
