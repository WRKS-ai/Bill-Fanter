FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get access once I join?
After you reach out and we get you set up, you'll receive a Discord invite and instructions to connect your account. From there you can jump into the alerts, daily calls, and community channels immediately.
Do I need to have taken the masterclass first?
No. Plenty of members join the community first to see the alerts and conversations in action, then decide whether they want to go deeper with the masterclass. Both paths work — pick what fits your situation.
How often are alerts posted?
Throughout every market session. Volume varies with conditions — quieter days see fewer alerts; high-volatility weeks can see several per session from me and the analyst team combined.
Is this just signals, or do you teach too?
Both. Every alert comes with the reasoning behind it, and the community has dedicated channels for video tutorials, trade reviews, and strategy discussions. The goal is for you to eventually not need the alerts at all.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Membership is flexible — reach out via the contact page if you have questions about pricing tiers or want to change your plan.
Is trading risky?
Yes — all trading involves risk, including the potential loss of principal. The community is built around teaching risk management as the first habit, so you trade with rules rather than vibes.
